Net Area
$$\displaylines{ A_n=A_g-(\,\textsf{Diameter of bolt}+\frac{1''}{8})\\\times\,\textsf{Thickness of plate}\\\\ A_n=A_g-(\,\textsf{Actual hole diameter}+\frac{1''}{16})\\\times\,\textsf{Thickness of plate}\\\\ \textsf{Effect of Staggered Holes on Net Area}\\ A_n=A_g-n\,(\,h\,t\,)+\frac{s^2}{4\,g}\,t }$$

Effective Net Area
$$\displaylines{ A_e=U\,A_n\\ U=1-\frac{\bar{x}}{L}\le 0.9 }$$

Design Strength
$$\displaylines{ \phi_t\,T_n\ge T_u=1.2\,DL+1.6\,LL\\\\ \textsf{Yielding in gross section}\\ \phi_t\,T_n=\phi_t\,F_y\,A_g=0.9\,F_y\,A_g\\\\ \textsf{Fracture in net section}\\ \phi_t\,T_n=\phi_t\,F_u\,A_e=0.75\,F_u\,A_e\\\\ \textsf{(Smaller control)} }$$

Tearing Failure at Bolt Holes
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Shear Yielding - Tension Fracture}\\ (\,F_u\,A_{nt}\ge 0.6\,F_u\,A_{nv}\,)\\ T_n=0.6\,F_y\,A_{gv}+F_u\,A_{nt}\\\\ \textsf{Shear Fracture - Tension Yielding}\\ (\,F_u\,A_{nt}\lt 0.6\,F_u\,A_{nv}\,)\\ T_n=0.6\,F_u\,A_{nv}+F_y\,A_{gt}\\\\ T_n=\textsf{nominal strength of a tension member}\\ A_{gv}=\textsf{gross area acted upon by shear}\\ A_{gt}=\textsf{gross area acted upon by tension}\\ A_{nv}=\textsf{net area acted upon by shear}\\ A_{nt}=\textsf{net area acted upon by tension}\\ F_u=\textsf{minimum tensile strength}\\ F_y=\textsf{minimum yield stress} }$$

Fillet Welds
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{From Figure 5.11.2},\\ t\lt\frac{1''}{4}\Rightarrow a_{\textsf{max}}=t\\ t\ge\frac{1''}{4}\Rightarrow a_{\textsf{max}}=t-\frac{1''}{16}\\ \textsf{Minimum a}\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 5.11.1}\\\\ \textsf{Effective throat dimension},\,t_e\\ \textsf{Except SAW},\,t_e=0.707\,a\\\\ \textsf{For SAW},\\ a\le\frac{3}{8}\Rightarrow t_e=a\\ a\gt\frac{3}{8}\Rightarrow t_e=0.707\,a+0.11\\\\ \textsf{For fillet weld},\\ \phi\,R_{nw}=\phi\,t_e\,(\,0.6\,F_{EXX}\,)\\\\ \textsf{For base metal},\\ \phi\,R_{nw}=\phi\,t\,(\,0.6\,F_u\,)\\\\ F_{EXX}\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 5.13.1}\\ t=\textsf{thickness of base material}\\ F_u=\textsf{tensile strength of base metal}\\\\ \textsf{The strength provided by fillet weld},\\ T=L_w\,\phi\,R_{nw}\\ \textsf{The strength provided by plug/slot weld},\\ T=\phi\,R_{nw}=0.75\times\textsf{faying area}\times 0.6\,F_{EXX}\\\\ \textsf{Tensile capacity of plate}\\ \phi\,T_n=0.9\,F_y\,A_g\\ \phi\,T_n=0.75\,F_u\,A_e\\ A_e=U\,A_n=1\times A_n=A_g\\ \phi\,T_n\ge T_u\\\\ \textsf{Required }L_w=\frac{T_u}{\phi\,R_{nw}} }$$
