Seismic Load

Note
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Use tables from Myanmar National Building}\\\textsf{Code (MNBC).} }$$

Spectral Response Acceleration Parameters
$$\displaylines{ S_S = \textsf{short-period (0.2 s) spectral acceleration}\\ S_1 = \textsf{1-second spectral acceleration}\\ S_{MS}\textsf{ and }S_{M1} = \textsf{maximum spectral acceleration}\\ S_{DS}\textsf{ and }S_{D1} = \textsf{design spectral acceleration}\\\\ S_S\textsf{ and }S_1\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 3.4.1}\\ \textsf{Site Classification}\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 3.4.2 and 3.4.5}\\\\ S_{MS}=F_a\,S_S\\ S_{M1}=F_v\,S_1\\\\ \textsf{Site Coefficients \(F_a\) and \(F_v\)}\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 3.4.3 and 3.4.4}\\\\ S_{DS}=\frac{2}{3}\,S_{MS}\\ S_{D1}=\frac{2}{3}\,S_{M1}\\\\ \textsf{Seismic design category (SDC)}\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 3.4.7 and 3.4.8}\\ }$$

Seismic Base Shear
$$\displaylines{ V=C_s\,W\\\\ C_s=\frac{S_{DS}}{R/I}\\\\ C_s= \textsf{the seismic response coefficient}\\ W = \textsf{the effective seismic weight}\\ R = \textsf{the response modification factor}\\ R\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 3.4.9 and 9.5.2.2}\\ I = \textsf{the occupancy importance factor}\\\\ \textsf{From ASCE 7-02,}\\ \textsf{Occupancy Category}\\\Downarrow\\ \textsf{Seismic Use Group (Table 9.1.3)}\\\Downarrow\\ \textsf{Occupancy Importance Factor (Table 9.1.4)} }$$

Vertical Lateral Force
$$\displaylines{ F_x=C_{vx}\times V\\\\ \textsf{Check Fundamental Period,}\,T_a\\ T_a=0.016\,h_n^{0.9}\quad\textsf{(For R.C.C)}\\ h_n=\textsf{height from ground to roof beam}\\ T_a\lt T_L\quad\textsf{(OK)}\\ T_L=6\,sec\Rightarrow\textsf{Table 3.4.1}\\\\ \textsf{Distributed Load to Each Floor}\\ C_{vx}=\frac{w_x\,h_x^k}{\sum_{i=1}^n w_i\,h_i^k}\\\\ T_a\lt 0.5\,sec\Rightarrow k=1\\ T_a\ge 2.5\,sec\Rightarrow k=2\\ 0.5\lt T_a\lt 2.5\Rightarrow k=\textsf{interpolation} }$$

Vertical Seismic Load
$$\displaylines{ E_v=0.2\,S_{DS}\times D\\ S_{DS}=\textsf{seismic parameter}\\ D=\textsf{dead load} }$$
