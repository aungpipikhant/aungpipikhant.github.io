Hydraulic Design of Sewers

Hydraulic Elements of Circular Sewers
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Central Angle}\\ \cos\frac{1}{2}\theta=1-\frac{2\,d}{D}\\\\ \textsf{Depth}\\ d=\frac{D}{2}\left(\,1-\cos\frac{\theta}{2}\,\right)\\\\ \textsf{Area}\\ a=\frac{\pi D^2}{4}\left[\,\frac{\theta}{360°}-\frac{\sin\theta}{2\pi}\,\right]\\\\ \textsf{Proportional Area}\\ \frac{a}{A}=\left[\,\frac{\theta}{360°}-\frac{\sin\theta}{2\pi}\,\right]\\\\ \textsf{Wetted Perimeter}\\ p=\pi D\,\frac{\theta}{360°}\\\\r=\frac{a}{p}\quad\quad q=\frac{1}{N}\,a\,r^{\frac{2}{3}}S^{\frac{1}{2}}\\\\ \textsf{When the sewer runs full, }R=\frac{D}{4}\\ A=\frac{\pi D^2}{4}\quad\quad P=\pi D\\\\ \textsf{Proportional Discharge}\\ \frac{q}{Q}=\frac{\theta}{360°}\left[\,1-\frac{360°\sin\theta}{2\pi\theta}\,\right]^{\frac{5}{3}}\\\\ \textsf{Proportional Velocity}\\ \frac{v}{V}=\left[\,1-\frac{360°\sin\theta}{2\pi\theta}\,\right]^{\frac{2}{3}}\\\\ \textsf{Proportional Hydraulic Mean Depth}\\ \frac{r}{R}=\left[\,1-\frac{360°\sin\theta}{2\pi\theta}\,\right]\\\\ \frac{q_{\textsf{max}}}{Q}=\frac{v}{V}\times\frac{a}{A}\\\\ Q=\frac{1}{N}\,A\,R^{\frac{2}{3}}\,S^{\frac{1}{2}}\\ V=\frac{1}{N}\,R^{\frac{2}{3}}\,S^{\frac{1}{2}}\\\\ \frac{v}{V}=\frac{N}{n}\left(\frac{r}{R}\right)^{\frac{2}{3}}\\\\ \frac{q}{Q}=\left(\frac{N}{n}\right)\left(\frac{a}{A}\right)\left(\frac{r}{R}\right)^{\frac{2}{3}} }$$

For Self-Cleansing Flow
$$\displaylines{ s_s=\left(\frac{R}{r}\right)S\\\\ v_s=\frac{N}{n}\left(\frac{r}{R}\right)^{\frac{1}{6}}V\\\\ q_s=\left(\frac{N}{n}\right)\left(\frac{a}{A}\right)\left(\frac{r}{R}\right)^{\frac{1}{6}}Q }$$
