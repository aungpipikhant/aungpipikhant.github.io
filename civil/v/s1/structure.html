$$\displaylines{ \textsf{The stiffness matrix for spring element is}\\ \hat{\underline{k}}=\begin{bmatrix} \,\,\,\,k & -k\,\,\,\\ -k & \,\,\,\,k\,\,\, \end{bmatrix}\\\\ \textsf{Global equation}\Rightarrow\underline{F}=\underline{K}\,\underline{d}\\ \underline{F}=\textsf{Global nodal force matrix}\\ \underline{K}=\textsf{Global stiffness matrix}\\ \underline{d}=\textsf{Global nodal displacement matrix}\\\\ \textsf{Local equation}\Rightarrow\hat{\underline{f}}=\hat{\underline{k}}\,\hat{\underline{d}}\\ \textsf{For element 1 between node 1 and 2},\\ \begin{Bmatrix} \hat{f}_{1x}\\ \hat{f}_{2x} \end{Bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} \,\,\,\,k_1 & -k_1\,\,\,\\ -k_1 & \,\,\,\,k_1\,\,\, \end{bmatrix} \begin{Bmatrix} \hat{d}_{1x}\\ \hat{d}_{2x} \end{Bmatrix} }$$

Displacement Method of Analysis

Stiffness Method
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Fixed-end forces \(\{\,F\,\}\) and \(\{\,A_r\,\}\)}\Rightarrow\textsf{Appendix C}\\\\ \textsf{End-forces caused by end-displacements}\\ [\,S\,],\,[\,A_u\,]\Rightarrow\textsf{Appendix D}\\\\ \{\,D\,\}=[\,S\,]^{-1}\{\,-F\,\}\\ \{\,A\,\}=\{\,A_r\,\}+[\,A_u\,]\{\,D\,\}\\\\ \{\,A\,\}=\textsf{final forces in the member}\\ \{\,A_r\,\}=\textsf{forces in the member}\\\quad\quad\textsf{in the restrained condition}\\ [\,A_u\,]=\textsf{forces in the member corresponding}\\\textsf{to unit displacement}\\ }$$

Notes

Structural Dynamics

Notes
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Mass},\,m=\frac{W}{g}\\ \textsf{Stiffness},\,S=K\Rightarrow\textsf{Appendix E}\\ \textsf{Natural angular frequency},\,\omega=\sqrt{\frac{S}{m}}\\ \textsf{Natural period of vibration},\,T=\frac{2\,\pi}{\omega}\\ \textsf{Amplitude},\,\bar{D}=D_{\text{max}}=\sqrt{D_0^2+\left(\,\frac{\dot{D}_0}{\omega}\,\right)^2}\\ \textsf{Displacement},\,D_t=\frac{\dot{D}_0}{\omega}\,\sin\omega\,t+D_0\,\cos\omega\,t\\ D_0=\textsf{Initial displacement}\\ \dot{D}_0=\textsf{Velocity}\\\\ m_1=m_2=m,\\ [\,m\,]=\begin{bmatrix} m_1 & 0\\ 0 & m_2 \end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} m & 0\\ 0 & m \end{bmatrix}=m \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0\\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\\\\ [\,m\,]^{-1}=\frac{1}{m}\,\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0\\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}\\\\ [\,B\,]=[\,m\,]^{-1}[\,S\,]\\\\ \left|\,[\,B\,]-\omega^2\,[\,I\,]\,\right|=0\\\\ \left|\,[\,B\,]-\omega^2\,[\,I\,]\,\right|\,\{\,D\,\}=0\\\\ I=\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0\\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} }$$
