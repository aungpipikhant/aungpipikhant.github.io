Chapter 7

Road Estimating

Earthwork

Method I
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Quantity}=(\,Bd+Sd^2\,)\times\textsf{length}\\ d=\textsf{mean depth} }$$

Method II
$$\displaylines{ A_1=\textsf{Sec. area at one end}=Bd_1+sd_1^2\\ A_2=\textsf{Sec. area at other end}=Bd_2+sd_2^2\\ \textsf{Mean Sec. area}=\frac{A_1+A_2}{2}\\ \textsf{Quantity}=\textsf{Mean Sec. area}\times\textsf{Length} }$$

Method III
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Prismoidal Formula}\\\\ \textsf{Quantity}=\frac{L}{6}\,(\,A_1+A_2+4A_m\,)\\ A_m=\textsf{Mid. Sec. area} }$$

Notes
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Central area}=Bd\\ \textsf{Area of sides}=sd^2\\ \textsf{Total sec. area}=Bd+sd^2\\ \textsf{Quantity}=(\,Bd+sd^2\,)\times L }$$

Hill Road
$$\displaylines{ \textsf{Formula for sectional area},\\ A=\frac{sb^2+r^2\,(\,2bd+sd^2\,)}{r^2-s^2}\\\\ b=\frac{B}{2} }$$
